Prologue:

    You are trapped inside the tower with mysterious power at the first floor. You need to fight your way through lots of guard in the first floor by eliminating all of them and go to second floor. At second floor, you will need to find a total of 3 keys hidden on that level in order to make a portal to the third floor. Finally, you will have to face with the owner of the tower in face-to-face.

    Reminder: In order to eliminate your foes, there is only one way which is winning in "RPS", or Rock-Paper-Scissor game. For each time you win, loser lose 10 HP. If you lose, you lose 5 HP. There is no consequence of a draw match.
